DIVERTIDAS

Canada |

2025 |

2 hours |

English

Wednesday, july 14

Vancouver Art Gallery

9:00 pm

A deep-space research vessel, Hermes, receives a mysterious signal from beyond the known universe. Leading the mission is Dr. Elia Voss, a brilliant yet haunted scientist determined to uncover the truth behind the anomaly. When the crew ventures into the uncharted Void Sector, they discover an ancient structure pulsing with alien technology—evidence of a long-lost civilization that may have seeded life across the stars. But as the signal grows stronger, the crew begins to experience visions, twisted memories, and a growing sense that something is watching them. Elia soon realizes the signal is not a call for help—it’s a warning. As time unravels and reality fractures, the team must confront a force that defies space, time, and logic. "Echoes Beyond" is a visually stunning, mind-bending journey through the limits of science, the fragility of memory, and the deep unknowns of our place in the cosmos.
